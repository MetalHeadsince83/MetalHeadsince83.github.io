St. Anger

2003, the year Metallica kicks off the 2000's by releasing "St. Anger" St. Anger is a Metallica album and song that reflects the personal turmoil and the artistic vision of the band. The song is about being angry and frustrated with one's partner and wanting to let go of the negative emotions. Many fans have some mixed feelings about this album. Some fans like it, while others dont. But either way this song has some heavy hitters like its title track "St. Anger" and "Frantic" and "Shoot Me Again"
