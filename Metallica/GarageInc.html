Garage, Inc.

1998 was the year Metallica had released Garage, Inc. But unlike the albums before it, Garage, Inc. had been labeled as a compilation album, that's because Most of their songs were songs that were either songs that didn't make it on their past albums, or songs that they covered from other artists, with a total of 27 songs.
