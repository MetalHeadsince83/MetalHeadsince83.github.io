HardWired...To Self Destruct

2016, a whole 8 years until Metallica came back with their next album "Hardwired... To Self Destruct" The title, "Hardwired... to Self-Destruct", came from an utterance Hetfield heard. "Somebody said to me the other day, and it stuck with me, 'Hardwired... Hardwired to Self-Destruct'. So it's like no matter what you do in your life you're really trying to not go down that path that you're maybe hard-wired to do." This theme unites disparate topics such as the dangers of fame on "Moth Into Flame" and cosmicism in "Dream No More".
