Master Of Puppets

1998 was the year Metallica had released "Master Of Puppets" The album "Master of Puppets" by Metallica has lyrics that are from the point of view of a personification of addiction. The "Master" in the title refers to drugs, which control the drug user's life. The lyrics describe the manipulation and control exerted by addiction, with lines like "The Master Of Puppets is pulling your strings, twisting your mind and smashing your dreams" The album explores the theme of manipulation by "the invisible forces of control that govern all our lives" This album brought more heavy hitters like "Battery" "Master Of Puppets" and "Welcome Home (Sanatarium)"
