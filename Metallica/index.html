Metallica

Since 1981, Metallica has rocked around the world, starting from its roots with their first album "Kill 'Em All" from 1983, and all the way to their latest album "72 Seasons" that came out in 2023. Over the years, Metallica as a band has faced some challenges, like losing their first bass player in a accident in 1986, to their second bass player leaving the band in 2001, to some legal trouble, and to some badass songs on some badass albums, this is the history of the thrash metal band, Metallica
