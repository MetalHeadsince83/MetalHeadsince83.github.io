72 Seasons

We made it, 2023, a whole 7 year wait, Metallica released "72 Seasons" James has said, the album title refers to a human's formative years. A charred cot occupies the centre of the image and is surrounded by fire-damaged detritus of a pre-adult life: soft toys, a tricycle, wooden toys, a guitar, a baseball bat and a pile of empty cans. 72 Seasons received generally positive reviews, with critics singling out Hetfield's lyricism for praise, though the album's length was criticized.
